Software

I maintain a strong knowledge and skill set in ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) systems that centers around product management and website data. I have an introductory skill set that I continue to work upon in CRM (Customer Relationship Management) software, such as Salesforce or HubSpot.

Webdesign

I maintain a strong skill set in Bootstrap which enables the development of responsive, mobile-first websites with a streamlined design, ensuring compatibility across various devices. My expertise in BigCommerce and WordPress allows for the creation and management of e-commerce sites and content-rich platforms, with the ability to customize themes and optimize user experience. Proficiency in HTML and CSS forms the foundation for building structured, well-styled web pages, ensuring clean code and seamless layouts. Together, these skills facilitate the creation of modern, functional websites that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

Adobe

I am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, I have experience with photo manipulation, digital graphics, and advanced techniques like masking, layering, and retouching to create professional-grade designs. In Adobe Acrobat, I am skilled at creating, editing, and optimizing PDFs, including adding annotations, form fields, and securing documents with password protection.

Creativity plays a key role in my work, allowing me to think outside the box, approach problems innovatively, and deliver fresh, impactful solutions that drive success in both individual and group settings.
